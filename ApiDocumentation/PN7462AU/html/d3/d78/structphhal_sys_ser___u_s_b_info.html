§ USBDesc_Attributes

uint8_t phhalSysSer_USBInfo::USBDesc_Attributes[1]

Self Powered or BusPowerd [7..0].

  • Bit [0-4] Reserved. Should be 0
  • Bit 5 - Remote Wakeup
  • Bit 6 - 1 = Self powered, 0 = Bus Powered
  • Bit 7 - Reserved. Should be 1

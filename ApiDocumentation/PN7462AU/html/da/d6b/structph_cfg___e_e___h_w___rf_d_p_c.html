§ wControlCycle

uint16_t phCfg_EE_HW_RfDPC::wControlCycle

Sets the value for the periodic regulation.

Time base is 1/20Mhz. (Example: Value of 20000 is equal to 1ms)

§ wGuardTimeFastMode

uint16_t phCfg_EE_HW_RfDPC::wGuardTimeFastMode

Guard time after AGC fast mode has been triggered.

This happens in the following scenarios:

  • End of Receive
  • End of Transmit
  • After a gear switch Time base is 1/20MHz (Example: Value of 2000 is equal to 100us)

§ wGuardTimeSofDetected

uint16_t phCfg_EE_HW_RfDPC::wGuardTimeSofDetected

Guard time after SoF or SC detection.

This is to avoid any DPC regulation between SoF/SC and actual begin of reception. Time base is 1/20MHz (Example: Value of 2000 is equal to 100us)

§ wGuardTimeFieldOn

uint16_t phCfg_EE_HW_RfDPC::wGuardTimeFieldOn

Guard time after Gear Switch during FieldOn instruction.

Time base is 1/20MHz (Example: Value of 2000 is equal to 100us)
